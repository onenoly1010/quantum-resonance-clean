## Overview

The Quantum Resonance Forge operates on a rhythmic cadence of rituals that maintain system health, community alignment, and technical excellence.

## Daily Rituals

### Morning Resonance (09:00 UTC)
**Duration**: 30 minutes  
**Participants**: All Guardians  
**Frequency**: Every day

**Agenda**:
1. Review overnight telemetry and alerts
2. Check system health metrics
3. Verify wallet balance and transactions
4. Review open issues and PRs
5. Set daily priorities

**Deliverables**:
- Daily health check report
- Priority task list
- Blocking issues identified

---

### Evening Broadcast (21:00 UTC)
**Duration**: 15 minutes  
**Participants**: Broadcaster, Primary Guardian  
**Frequency**: Every day

**Agenda**:
1. Publish daily telemetry summary
2. Broadcast completed work
3. Update community on progress
4. Share key metrics

**Deliverables**:
- Daily broadcast message
- Telemetry summary report
- Community update

---

## Ritual Participation Guidelines

### Attendance
- **Required**: Daily rituals for assigned roles
- **Recommended**: Weekly and bi-weekly rituals
- **Optional**: Monthly celebrations (but encouraged!)

### Preparation
- Review agenda 24 hours before ritual
- Complete assigned pre-work
- Bring questions and insights
- Come with positive energy

### Documentation
- All rituals have notes
- Action items tracked in GitHub Issues
- Decisions recorded in ADR (Architecture Decision Records)
- Celebrate wins publicly

### Time Zones
- All times in UTC
- Use [time zone converter](https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html)
- Recordings available for async participation

## Adapting the Calendar

The ritual calendar is a living document:

- **Propose changes**: Open an issue with tag `ritual-calendar`
- **Trial period**: New rituals tested for 1 month
- **Community input**: Gather feedback during Full Moon Assembly
- **Refinement**: Adjust based on team needs and feedback
